How can you be sure that the suspicious email your boss sent you was real?

How can you tell if the photo being sent to you over IM is really being sent by your mate?

TrustMe tells you who you can trust.

Trust-ing a friend is a simple three step process.

  • Tap your phone against a friend's
  • Interact with your phone to assign them one of four trust levels provided
  • Share your TrustKey with friends so that they can trust you as well
Now whenever you receive an SMS, Email or other wireless communication, TrustMe will let you know if it's safe, and from who it seems to be from.
